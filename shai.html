Shai Resource Reserve

Shai Resource Reserve is a resource reserve located in Doryumu in the Shai Osudoku District all in the Greater Accra Region of southern Ghana. it lies along the Tema-Akosombo Highway some 57km from Accra. The protected area was home to the Shai people before they were ejected by the British in 1892. Remains of the Shai people's work can still be found in the reserve. The reserve was established in 1962 with area of 47 kilometer square which was later extended to 51 kilometer square in 1973. It was made a Game Production Reserve in 1971. The Shai Hills Reserve is covered with grassland and low dry forest vegetation. There are nearly 400 plant species spread on the five separate hlls at the Shai Hills Resorce Reserve.
