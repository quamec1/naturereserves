    • Paga Crocodile Pond

    Paga Crocodile Pond is a sacred pond in Paga in the Upper East Region of Ghana, and is 44 kilometers outside Bolgatanga, the regional capital. It is inhabited by wild West African Crocodiles, with some up to 90 years old. Due to the friendliness of the reptiles, it has become popular among tourists and the pond is now reliant on tourism to ensure the population of crocodiles remain fed and healthy. It is also known as Chief's pond.
